About plantyboi

Plantyboi was created at the behest of a bunch of planty questions being fielded by Breamons from all the planty parents who were her friends and coworkers. After turning her grey cubicle into a veritable plantyjungle, passers by kept on coming with their crispy leaves,and moldy roots, and other maladies for healing by the original plantyparent. After fixing some problems and helping turn some black thumbs into green ones, Breamons decided to keep track of, not only her plantyfriends, but the plantyanswers and scenarios. So here you are. The plantyknowledge dump. Plants love a good compost pile.
